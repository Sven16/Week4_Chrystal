Crystals Collector!

How to play:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of poiints to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randon number, you lose if your total score totals more than the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

The value of each crystal changes every time the game starts.
